This term in Physical Education, students will be focusing on being able to identify and perform activites that emphasise two of the health-related fitness components: muscular strength and endurance. Muscular strength and endurance helps control weight by increasing metabolism, reduces the risk of one's injury, leads to healthier, stronger muscles and bones and also increases one's ability to do activities without getting tired. Students will be taught how to effectively create a training routine to increase muscular strength and endurance.
